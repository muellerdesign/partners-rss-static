Who We Are

Research Support Services (RSS) was created in 2014 to help support the infrastructure of Partners HealthCare, recipient of the most federal NIH funding dollars worldwide.

RSS is equipped to offer research administrative support to investigators, departments and organizations, no matter where they may be.
